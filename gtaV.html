Grand Theft Auto V

Rockstar North

Offical Website
  • Grand Theft Auto V is a 2013 action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games.
  • Set within the fictional state of San Andreas, based on Southern California, the single-player story follows three protagonists—retired bank robber Michael De Santa, street gangster Franklin Clinton, and drug dealer and arms smuggler Trevor Philips—and their attempts to commit heists while under pressure from a corrupt government agency and powerful criminals.
  • The open world design lets players freely roam San Andreas' open countryside and the fictional city of Los Santos, based on Los Angeles.
  • The game broke industry sales records and became the fastest-selling entertainment product in history, earning $800 million in its first day and $1 billion in its first three days.
